The basic strategy to play control decks, is not to go to the opponent's face until the late game. If you break your opponent's runes till that moment, he will have more cards in his hands to play against you. So, you have to avoid that situation. In each turn you must limit yourself to remove any opponent's threats and you have only to preserve your life points. While you are reaching twelve magicka, you will gradually gain more and more strength and as your opponent remains with only one card in his hands, you will have meantime gradually gained the control of the whole board and you will have certainly played the number of creatures that can let you go to your opponent's face. Only at that moment you will be certainly able to win the game.	</div>
